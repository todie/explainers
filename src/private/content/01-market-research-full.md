# Market Research Brief: The Case for unsigned.gg's Revenue-Floor Model

> unsigned.gg's "generous revenue floor" — letting founders keep 100% of revenue until they hit a personal income threshold — directly addresses the single biggest structural barrier keeping technical talent from going full-time on startups. With **5.2 million new businesses formed annually** in the US, **44% launched by people still employed**, and **99.95% of startups never raising venture capital**, the market for a GTM-for-equity studio is enormous and growing.

---

## 1. The Bootstrapped & Pre-Seed Landscape

The Census Bureau recorded **5,479,144 new business applications in 2023** (a record). **80.9% of new entrepreneurs are "opportunity entrepreneurs"** (Kauffman). **44% of founders launched while working for another employer** (Gusto 2024). Only **0.05% of startups** ever raise VC. **78% are entirely self-funded** (SCORE). Bootstrapped startups are **3x more likely to be profitable** within three years (CB Insights). Global VC funding fell **54%** from $681B (2021) to $314B (2024). Only **20% of the 2022 seed cohort** graduated to Series A. YC acceptance rate: **0.6%** for S25.

## 2. Revenue Thresholds & The Economics of Quitting

| MRR Range | Significance |
| --- | --- |
| $3K–$5K | "Ramen profitable" — covers basics |
| $5K–$8K | Sweet spot for quit consideration |
| $10K ($120K ARR) | "Escape velocity" — full-time threshold |
| $15K | Equivalent to a good tech job |

Founder salary gap: bootstrapped median **$60K** vs seed VC-backed **$147K** (Pilot/Kruze 2025). Senior FAANG comp: $350K–$500K+ with **~$1.2M unvested equity**. **47% cite unvested equity** as top reason they stay (Blind 2023).

## 3. Academic Evidence: Hybrid Entrepreneurship

**Raffiee & Feng (2014, AMJ)**: Hybrid entrepreneurs who transition to full-time have **much higher survival rates**. **Folta et al. (2010, MS)**: Hybrid entrepreneurs **28.8x more likely** to enter full-time. **Loss aversion** (Kahneman): Losses weighted **~2x** over equivalent gains.

## 4. Venture Studio Landscape

**800–1,100 active studios globally** (625% growth, InNiches 2024). Studio IRR: **53%** vs 21.3% traditional VC. **No studio uses milestone-based equity WITH a revenue floor.**

## 5. Total Addressable Market

~5.2M new businesses/year → ~1.66M tech → ~730K started while employed → ~591K opportunity entrepreneurs → **6K–30K viable pre-seed technical founders**. Serviceable market: **$300M–$6B**.

## 6. Conclusion

The $5K–$10K MRR zone is the critical intervention point. The golden handcuffs gap is quantifiable. Academic literature provides peer-reviewed empirical evidence. unsigned.gg is operationalizing validated theory.
