## Deal Structure

### What the Studio Provides

| Service | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Go-to-Market Strategy** | Full GTM planning: positioning, messaging, channel strategy, launch execution |
| **Marketing Execution** | Content creation, social media, SEO, community building, paid campaigns |
| **Marketing Channel Spend** | Studio covers all paid ad spend (LinkedIn, X/Twitter, Google, etc.) |
| **Infrastructure** | Shared platform services via unsigned-paas (hosting, CI/CD, monitoring) |
| **Technical Advisory** | Architecture review, technical co-founding support as needed |

### What the Studio Receives

**Milestone-based equity** in the Company, earned incrementally by hitting revenue targets. No equity is granted upfront.

---

## Equity Schedule

| MRR Target | Cumulative Equity Vested |
| --- | --- |
| $2,500 | 5% |
| $5,000 | 10% |
| $7,500 | 15% |
| $10,000+ | 20% (cap) |

* **Hard cap:** 20% total equity to Studio
* **Measurement:** MRR as reported by Stripe, Paddle, or agreed billing system
* **Vesting window:** 24 months from execution. Unvested equity expires at window close.
* **Anti-dilution:** Standard weighted-average through Series A

---

## Founder Protection: Revenue Floor

| Phase | Founder Revenue Floor | Studio Distribution |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Growth Phase** | $0 – $7,500/mo net | Founder keeps 100% of all revenue |
| **Sharing Phase** | Above $7,500/mo net | Profit-sharing kicks in |

The floor ensures the Founder can sustain themselves before the Studio participates in upside.

---

## Profit Sharing

Once the Company exceeds the Founder Revenue Floor:

* **Split:** 70/30 (Founder/Studio) on revenue above floor
* **Example:** Floor $7,500, month does $12,000 → $4,500 surplus split 70/30 = $3,150 Founder, $1,350 Studio
* **Cap adjustment:** Percentages may adjust downward as MRR grows (e.g., 80/20 above $25K MRR)

---

## Service Level Agreement (SLA)

| Metric | Minimum |
| --- | --- |
| **Marketing hours/week** | 15 hrs dedicated |
| **Campaign cadence** | 1 campaign launch per month |
| **Reporting** | Monthly growth report (CAC, LTV, activation, retention, MRR) |
| **Strategy reviews** | Biweekly sync |
| **Response time** | 24hr on critical issues |

If Studio fails SLA for **2 consecutive months**, Founder may renegotiate, pause vesting, or terminate.

---

## Buyout Clause

* **Buyout price:** 3x trailing 12-month profit-share earnings, or FMV of equity (whichever higher)
* **Right of first refusal:** Studio has 30-day ROFR on third-party offers
* **Clean-cap-table provision:** Payment structured over 12 months if triggered by fundraising

---

## Term & Termination

* **Initial term:** 24 months
* **Termination:** 60-day written notice; Studio retains vested equity + 90-day profit-share tail
* **Termination for cause:** Immediate if material breach

---

## Additional Terms

* **IP:** All Company IP remains 100% owned by the Company
* **Non-compete:** Studio will not serve direct competitors during term without consent
* **Confidentiality:** Standard mutual NDA
