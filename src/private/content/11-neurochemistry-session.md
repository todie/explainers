## Medication Stack

* **Atomoxetine** — selective NRI, NE elevated, no DA activity
* **Lamotrigine** — sodium channel / glutamate modulator, activating, DA-benign
* **Oxcarbazepine** — CYP3A4 inducer — likely reducing effective lamotrigine plasma levels
* **Net effect:** NE elevated, DA suppressed, reward ceiling lowered → affect blunting + motivation deficit overdetermined
