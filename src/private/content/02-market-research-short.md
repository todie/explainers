# Market Research: Revenue Floor as Founder De-risking Mechanism

### unsigned.gg Venture Studio — April 2026

---

## Executive Summary

The revenue floor embedded in unsigned.gg's GTM-for-equity term sheet is not a novelty — it is a direct response to a documented behavioral and structural barrier in early-stage company formation. This brief synthesizes market data supporting the thesis that **a generous revenue cliff is the single most effective mechanism for unlocking committed, full-time founders from the employed talent pool**.

---

## 1. The Market Conditions Driving Demand

### Pre-seed funding is contracting while requirements are rising

* Pre-seed startups are **increasingly required to show revenue before raising** — a bar previously reserved for Seed stage. *(Aurelia Ventures / PitchBook, 2024)*
* Nearly **45% of rounds were under $250K** in Q3 2025, up from 39% prior quarter. *(Equidam)*
* **Bootstrapping trend surged 180%** — from 4.5% of founders in 2022 to 11.5% in 2023. *(Pilot)*

**Implication:** More technical founders are building without capital, staying in day jobs longer, and reaching first revenue with no structural support for making the quit leap.

---

## 2. The Behavioral Problem: The Quit Decision

The quit decision is not a capital problem — it is a **behavioral economics problem**. Founders delay because:

1. **Income replacement risk** is existential at the household level
2. **Opportunity cost anchoring** to current salary makes the math feel impossible
3. **Absence of a credible revenue signal** means the quit decision has no natural trigger

---

## 3. The Revenue Floor Benchmark

| Stage | Typical Founder Salary Range | Source |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Pre-seed (bootstrapped) | $0 – $75K | Pilot, 2023 |
| Pre-seed (VC-backed) | $40K – $75K | OpenVC, 2024 |
| Seed | $75K – $100K | Kruze Consulting |
| Series A | $125K – $175K | First Round Capital |

**The quit decision occurs between $5K–$10K MRR ($60K–$120K ARR)** for a founder with a standard tech salary. This maps directly to unsigned.gg's milestone schedule ($2.5K → $5K → $7.5K → $10K MRR).
