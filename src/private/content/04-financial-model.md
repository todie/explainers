## The Honest Math

Cash break-even on profit-share alone takes years. The real return is **equity value at exit**. This means:

1. Pick founders who can reach $10K MRR — that's the floor for a good bet
2. The home runs ($5M+ exits) subsidize the losses — portfolio diversification matters
3. Don't take on company #3 until company #1 is profit-sharing — cash flow discipline
