# Portfolio Intake Scorecard

**unsigned.gg — Founder Evaluation Rubric**
v1.0 — April 2026

Score each criterion 1–5. Both Christian and Jyovani score independently, then compare and discuss deltas > 1 point.

| Score | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| 5 | Exceptional — top 5% |
| 4 | Strong — clear evidence, high confidence |
| 3 | Solid — meets the bar, some unknowns |
| 2 | Weak — concerns, needs mitigation |
| 1 | Disqualifying — hard pass |

---

## Section 1: Founder (20 points max)

**1.1 Technical Ability** — Can this person actually build it?

**1.2 Execution Speed** — How fast do they ship? Visible trail of work?

**1.3 Coachability & Communication** — Will they take feedback? Are they responsive?

**1.4 Commitment Level** — Full-time focus or side project?

## Section 2: Product (20 points max)

**2.1 Problem Clarity** — Is the pain real and acute?

**2.2 Existing Traction** — Users, waitlist, revenue, LOIs?

**2.3 Defensibility** — What stops a bigger company from copying this?

**2.4 Market Size** — Is the TAM real and reachable?

## Section 3: Studio Fit (20 points max)

**3.1 GTM Leverage** — Can our marketing move the needle?

**3.2 Vertical Alignment** — AI/infra, dev tools, cybersecurity?

**3.3 Deal Fit** — Open to equity model?

**3.4 Time to Revenue** — Can this reach MRR within 12 months?

---

## Decision Framework

| Average Score | Decision |
| --- | --- |
| **48–60** | Strong yes — move to term sheet |
| **36–47** | Deeper diligence |
| **24–35** | Likely pass |
| **< 24** | Hard pass |

### Dealbreakers (auto-pass)

* Any criterion scored 1 by both evaluators
* Founder section average < 3.0
* Time to Revenue scored 1 or 2 by both
* Founder not willing to commit full-time within 90 days
